Carbon Offsetting Sites

Carbon offsetting is a way to compensate for your emissions by funding projects that reduce carbon dioxide in the atmosphere. The following platforms allow you to offset your carbon footprint while also earning rewards, cashback, or other incentives.

By using these services, you can make a positive impact on the environment while also benefiting financially.

Ecologi

Ecologi helps you offset your carbon footprint through verified tree planting and climate projects. Subscribe monthly and watch your virtual forest grow while earning impact rewards.

✓ Tree planting visualized
✓ Verified carbon projects
✓ Monthly impact reports
Visit Site

KlimaDAO

KlimaDAO turns your carbon reduction efforts into cryptocurrency rewards. Purchase carbon credits on the blockchain and earn KLIMA tokens that can be staked for additional returns.

✓ Crypto rewards (KLIMA tokens)
✓ Blockchain verified carbon credits
✓ Staking opportunities
Visit Site

Cloverly

Cloverly connects your everyday spending to carbon offsetting. Integrate with your business or use their API to automatically offset the carbon footprint of your operations and purchases.

✓ API for automatic offsetting
✓ Business integration
✓ Transaction-level impact
Visit Site

NCX

NCX offers a marketplace where landowners are paid to delay timber harvests, creating carbon credits you can purchase. Their Natural Capital Exchange creates transparent forest carbon credits.

✓ Forest carbon marketplace
✓ Transparent credit tracking
✓ Support for local landowners
Visit Site

TreeCard

TreeCard offers a wooden debit card that plants trees with merchant fees. Use their card for everyday purchases and they'll automatically fund reforestation projects with the interchange fees.

✓ Wooden debit card
✓ Zero user fees
✓ Tree planting tracker
Visit Site

Wren

Wren calculates your carbon footprint and helps you offset it through a monthly subscription. Your funds go to verified climate projects, and you'll receive regular updates on their progress.

✓ Carbon footprint calculator
✓ Monthly subscription
✓ Project updates & transparency
Visit Site

Patch

Patch is an API-first carbon credit marketplace focusing on frontier carbon removal technologies. Businesses can integrate carbon removal into their operations and offer it to customers.

✓ API integration
✓ Frontier carbon removal
✓ Business solutions
Visit Site

JustCarbon

JustCarbon tokenizes carbon removal units on the blockchain. Purchase carbon credits as digital tokens (JCR) and trade them on exchanges or hold them as an investment in climate action.

✓ Carbon credit tokenization
✓ Tradeable tokens
✓ Investment potential
Visit Site
